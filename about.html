Our Journey

Growth Timeline

From a one-person operation in Accra to a full-service digital agency — here's how we got here.

2020

🆕 Founded in Accra

SamzyDigital started as a one-person freelance operation. Samuel began building websites for local businesses from his desk in Accra with big ambitions and zero clients.

2021

🎉 First Major Client

Built a complete e-commerce platform for a local retailer — our first major project. Proof of concept that local businesses were ready to go digital.

2022

🤝 Community Launched

Created a free mentorship program for aspiring developers in Ghana. The SamzyDigital Community was born — now 100+ strong and growing every month.

2023

📈 15+ Projects Delivered

Reached 15+ completed websites and web apps for businesses across Ghana, including Ram's Beauty Salon — a landmark project with 200% booking increase.

2024

🚀 Team Expanded

Welcomed new developers and a UI/UX specialist. Launched automation and branding services. Now serving SMEs and growing enterprises across Accra and beyond.
