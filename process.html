The Process

Four Steps to Your Dream Website

Every SamzyDigital project follows the same proven process — so you always know what's happening and what comes next.

01
Listen & Discover

We start by understanding your business, your audience, your goals and your budget — before writing a single line of code.

02
Design & Plan

A tailored strategy, wireframes and clear project roadmap. You know exactly what you're getting, when, and for how much.

03
Build & Refine

04
Launch & Grow

We deploy, train your team, and continue supporting with SEO, updates and optimisations long after your site goes live.

Step One

Listen & Discover

Every great website starts with a great conversation. We take time to deeply understand your world before anything else.

  • Free 30–60 minute discovery call — no commitment required
  • We study your industry, competitors and target customers in Ghana
  • We review your existing digital presence and identify gaps
  • Budget discussion — we find a package that genuinely fits
  • Clear expectations set from day one — no hidden extras

Step Two

Design & Plan

We turn your goals into a clear, structured plan — wireframes, content structure, tech stack and a timeline you can count on.

  • Custom proposal delivered within 24 hours — full breakdown
  • Wireframes and design mockups shared for your approval
  • Content structure planned — copy, images and SEO strategy
  • Tech stack confirmed — what tools will power your site
  • 50% deposit to begin — balance only on completion

03
Step Three

Build & Refine

This is where the magic happens. We build fast, test thoroughly and involve you every step of the way.

  • Custom coded — no generic WordPress templates used
  • Weekly progress updates with live previews you can click through
  • Two rounds of revisions included before launch
  • Mobile-first design — perfect on every screen size
  • Speed optimised — fast loading even on Ghana's mobile data

Step Four

Launch & Grow

Going live is just the beginning. We stay by your side with support, training and ongoing growth strategies.

  • Full handover training — we teach you to manage your site
  • 30-day post-launch support included in every package
  • Google Search Console and Analytics setup and verification
  • SEO baseline — meta tags, schema markup and sitemap submitted
  • Optional maintenance retainer for ongoing updates and growth
