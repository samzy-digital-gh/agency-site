Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help us remember your login status and preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Authentication cookies: To keep you logged in as you navigate our site.
  • Session cookies: To remember your login session (they expire when you close your browser).
  • Persistence cookies: If you check "Remember me", we store a persistent cookie so you stay logged in across browser sessions.

We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

3. Firebase Cookies

Our Service uses Firebase Authentication, which may set its own cookies to manage authentication and session state. These are essential for the Service to function.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, if you disable cookies, some features of the Service (such as staying logged in) may not work properly.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block all cookies.
  • Delete all cookies when you close the browser.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
