5 Tasks Every Ghanaian Business Can Automate This Year — Save 10+ Hours a Week

Samuel AHIABA
Founder · SamzyDigital
December 2025 5 min read
Back to Blog

As a business owner in Ghana, your time is your most valuable asset. Yet so many of us spend hours on repetitive tasks that a computer could do better and faster.

Automation isn't about replacing people – it's about freeing up your team to focus on what really matters: serving customers and growing the business. Here are five tasks you can automate right now, using tools that work perfectly in Ghana.

BIGGEST TIME SAVER

The average Ghanaian SME owner spends 15+ hours a week on manual administrative tasks. Automating just three of these can give you back two full workdays every month.

1. Stock & Inventory Management

If you're still tracking stock with pen and paper or Excel, you're wasting hours. A simple inventory system can automatically:

  • Update stock levels when a sale is made (online or offline).
  • Send low‑stock alerts before you run out.
  • Generate purchase orders when inventory hits a reorder point.
  • Provide real‑time reports on best‑sellers and slow movers.

Tools we use: We build custom Supabase‑powered inventory dashboards for clients. For a simpler start, try Odoo or even an Excel sheet with macros.

2. Invoicing & Payment Reminders

Chasing payments is tedious and uncomfortable. Automated invoicing software can send invoices immediately, send polite reminders when a payment is due, and even apply late fees automatically.

How to do it: Use a platform like Hubtel, Paystack, or a CRM that integrates with MoMo. You can set up recurring invoices for subscription customers or membership payments.

3. Appointment Booking & Scheduling

If you run a service business (salon, clinic, consultancy), manual appointment scheduling is a bottleneck. An online booking system allows customers to:

  • View your real‑time availability and book 24/7.
  • Receive automatic confirmations and reminders via SMS or WhatsApp.
  • Reschedule or cancel without calling you.

Our approach: We integrate booking widgets directly into our clients' websites. For Ram's Beauty Salon, this alone cut admin time by 8 hours a week and increased bookings by 200%.

4. Customer Communication & Follow‑ups

Stay top‑of‑mind without manual effort. Automate:

  • Welcome emails when someone signs up or makes their first purchase.
  • Birthday offers using stored customer data.
  • Abandoned cart reminders for your e‑commerce store (dramatically increases recovery).
  • Review requests after a transaction – ask for a Google review automatically.

Tools: Many CRMs (like HubSpot or Mailchimp) offer these workflows. We also build custom automation using WhatsApp Business API for a more local touch.

5. Financial Reporting & Reconciliation

End‑of‑month accounting doesn't have to be a nightmare. Automate the collection of sales data from your website, MoMo transactions, and bank deposits into a single dashboard.

With tools like QuickBooks or even a custom dashboard, you can see your daily revenue, profit margins, and expense categories without manual data entry. Some platforms can even calculate tax obligations automatically.

Case Study: Mensah Retail Stock System

Mensah's retail shop in East Legon used to spend 12 hours a week manually counting stock, sending WhatsApp invoices, and chasing customers. We built them a custom automation platform that:

  • Integrates with their POS system (they still use a cash register).
  • Automatically updates stock levels and generates low‑stock alerts.
  • Sends automated invoices and payment reminders via WhatsApp.
  • Provides a real‑time dashboard showing sales, top products, and inventory value.

The result? They now spend just 2 hours a week on stock management, and their cash flow improved because customers pay faster with the automated reminders.
