Pricing & Payment
What things cost and how to pay in Ghana

Our packages start from GHS 1,500 for a simple one-page website all the way up to GHS 6,500+ for full e-commerce or web application builds. Every project is priced based on your specific needs — we never guess or overcharge. Book a free discovery call and we'll tell you exactly what your project will cost.

We accept MTN Mobile Money (MoMo), Vodafone Cash, bank transfer and Visa/Mastercard card payments. We make it easy to pay however works best for you. Our standard structure is a 50% deposit to kick off the project and 50% on completion before launch.

Timelines & Process
How long projects take and what the process looks like

Most standard websites go live in 7–14 days after the deposit is received and content is provided. E-commerce stores typically take 3–5 weeks, and full web applications with custom features take 6–10 weeks. We always give you a clear timeline upfront and stick to it.

We'll need your business information, logo (if you have one), any photos of your products or services, and an idea of what you want the site to do. Don't worry if you don't have everything — we can help with copywriting, stock images and even a basic logo to get you started.

Every project includes two full rounds of revisions after the initial design is shown to you. This covers changes to layout, colours, text and functionality. Major scope changes (like adding new sections or features not in the original proposal) may carry additional charges — but we'll always discuss this before proceeding.

We provide weekly progress updates via WhatsApp or email, share live preview links so you can see exactly what's being built, and are always available for questions. You're never left wondering what's happening with your project.
